About The Project

Empowering seamless communication through advanced computer vision

Why Iris?

Communication is a fundamental human right. Yet, millions of Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals face significant barriers in a hearing-dominant world. Iris is built to seamlessly translate American Sign Language (ASL) into spoken language, allowing for effortless, natural, and inclusive interactions.

Our system leverages state-of-the-art AI to interpret complex hand signals and motion patterns, transforming them into immediate speech output.

How It Works

A sophisticated pipeline running entirely in your browser

1. Capture

Your webcam captures real-time video feed of your sign language gestures.

2. Track

MediaPipe precisely maps hand landmarks and spatial geometry.

3. Analyze

Our ViViT transformer model decodes the temporal motion patterns.

4. Speak

The recognized sign is converted to natural human speech instantly.
